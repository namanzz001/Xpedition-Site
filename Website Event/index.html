About

Xpedition is a thrilling treasure hunt competition where selected teams will set out on an exhilarating mission. There will be an engaging plot that will enthrall every team. Teams will solve the larger mystery as they go along and uncover hidden clues that lead them to new places. The event is scheduled for August 27 at LP 108-109 at 2p.m . There will be eight levels and accordingly these eight clues will be placed at different locations in the college premises. Each team through their provided clue has to reach their designated mark. But it has an unconventional twist. This treasure hunt may not include any riches, but it does contain a bomb. The clues would eventually lead the teams to a bomb where they would have to figure out a way to defuse it, racing against time. So, do you have what it takes?! Come find out at our event THE XPEDITION and Unravel the enigma yourself.

Rulebook

Xpedition is a thrilling 'defuse the bomb' hunt with a time duration of two hours.
Teams qualifying the preliminary round will compete in the hunt.
Event will begin at 2:00 PM on 27th AUGUST, from LP 108, LP 109.
All teams will be registered on a separate discord channel to access the clues.
A story related to the event will be narrated to all teams, and clues will be sent to their respective discord channels accordingly.
There will be eight levels, with eight clues placed at different locations in the college premises. Each team must use their provided clue to reach their designated mark.
At each location, teams must search for a code and submit it on their respective discord channel. Once approved, they will receive further clues.
To defuse the bomb, teams must enter a code derived from all the other codes given.
The top three teams that successfully defuse the bomb will be declared as winners.
All participants must bring their mobile phones with reliable internet connectivity and have their own registered Discord ids.
Trading of clues between teams is strictly prohibited. Any team found doing so will be disqualified from the hunt.
